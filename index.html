I have been a part of Seattle for over 10 years and fell in love with its sense of community. I’ve loved learning from the friendly, honest and hard working people I’ve been blessed to know.

I value people above all else and want to include helping people as part of my journey. The uniqueness of each of my clients is very fulfilling for me, like walking through the beautiful garden of life.

Because of this, I decided to do business in a radically different way, sharing my gift of organizing while working in the abundance of love and believing in the goodness of others.

If I can help you move from burdened to blessed, please let me know!
